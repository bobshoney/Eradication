About Us

Who we are, how we operate

Eradication is a community of adults on US-Nightslayer who have been clearing raid content since Vanilla — and still show up on time with their flasks. Semi-dadcore: real jobs, real families, real responsibilities — and a full clear every lockout.

"Dad's home. Pull the boss."

Our raid leader has been calling pulls since Molten Core. That depth of experience means no wasted nights, no chaotic leadership, and no tolerance for nonsense. We run lean, we run clean, and we run with purpose. Three Karazhan teams, a progression 25-man, and a community that actually likes each other.

Loot System

Soft Reserve · MS > OS · Role Priority · Transparent

We run Soft Reserve (SR) with MS > OS via the Gargoyle addon. The SR link posts in #raid-info 24 hours before each raid. Every raider gets one soft reserve per lockout — use it wisely on your biggest upgrade.

1
Install Gargoyle — the SR link and instructions are pinned in #raid-info on Discord.
2
Claim your SR before raid invites go out. Late SR = no SR that lockout.
3
SR item drops: SR holder wins if it's their main spec. Ties broken by /roll.
4
Non-SR items: MS open-roll first. If no MS interest, OS rolls freely. No hoarding.
5
Reserved items are officer-distributed based on attendance, performance, and roster need.

SR managed via Gargoyle — link in #raid-info before every raid.
