About Us

Eradication is a community of experienced players on Nightslayer looking to conquer the burning fires of Outland. We are a semi-hardcore guild with a focus on efficient progression and a mature, low-drama atmosphere. Many of us have families, careers, and real-life commitments, but when raid time hits, we lock in to push content.

We pride ourselves on being a team where you can joke around between pulls but are expected to be fully prepared and focused when the boss is pulled. Our goal is to clear all TBC content at a solid pace while building a lasting community.

Optional Events

Alt raids, dungeon nights, and farming groups on off-nights. We keep the community active beyond raid nights.

Attendance

Consistency is key. We expect raiders to be on time and ready with consumables, enchants, and fight knowledge.

Loot System

We utilize a Loot Council system. Decisions are made based on what is best for the raid's overall progression, taking into account attendance, preparation, performance, and attitude. We use ThatsMyBis to track player wishlists and ensure transparency in our decision-making process.

Fairness and progression are our guiding principles.
