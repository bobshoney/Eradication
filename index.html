About Us

Who we are, how we operate

Eradication is a community of seasoned players on Nightslayer (US) dedicated to pushing through TBC content with intent and efficiency. We're semi-hardcore — adults with lives, careers, and families — but when the raid frames go up, we lock in.

"Joke between pulls. Lock in when it counts."

Our focus is clean, efficient progression without the toxic culture that kills guilds. We expect preparation, accountability, and a willingness to improve — and we'll offer the same in return.

Guild founded during Classic, veteran players with top-end TBC experience. We've been through the grind and built something worth logging in for every week.

Loot System

Fair, transparent, progression-minded

We use a Loot Council system managed through ThatsMyBis. All wishlist data is visible to raiders — there are no backroom deals. Loot decisions are made collaboratively by the officer team with the guild's long-term progression as the guiding principle.

  • Gear goes where it benefits the raid most — not to the loudest voice
  • Wishlist transparency via ThatsMyBis — your priorities are on record
  • Consistent raiders with high performance accumulate priority over time
  • New members enter a 2-week trial period before full loot eligibility
  • Disputes handled privately with officers — no drama on raid night
  • Disenchanting & sharding for the guild bank is always an option
