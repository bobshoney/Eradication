About Us

Who we are, how we operate

Eradication is a community of seasoned players on Nightslayer (US) built to push TBC content with intent and efficiency. We're semi-hardcore — adults with lives, careers, and families — but when the raid frames go up, we lock in completely.

"Joke between pulls. Lock in when it counts."

Our focus is clean, efficient progression without the toxic culture that destroys guilds. We demand preparation, accountability, and a drive to improve — and we give the same in return. Forged in Classic, battle-tested, and here for every phase of TBC.

⚔️
Progression First
Smart pulls, fast resets, relentless improvement. We log in to kill bosses.
🧠
Preparation
Consumable-ready, enchanted, fight knowledge locked. No hand-holding on pull.
🤝
Low Drama
Zero tolerance for toxicity. Strong community, mature culture — always.
📈
Growth Mindset
Parse reviews, coaching, continuous improvement. Team growth is personal growth.
👑
Veteran Officers
Leadership with real TBC experience and a bias toward clear communication.
🏆
Long Haul
We're here for every phase — T4 through Sunwell and beyond.

Loot System

Soft Reserve · MS > OS · Role Priority · Transparent

We run Soft Reserve (SR) with MS > OS using SoftReserve.tech. The SR link is posted in #raid-info 24 hours before each raid. Every raider gets one soft reserve per lockout.

1
Claim your SR via the SoftReserve link in #raid-info before the raid begins.
2
SR item drops: SR holder wins if it's their main spec. Ties broken by /roll.
3
Non-SR items: MS open-roll beats OS. If no MS interest, OS may roll freely.
4
Tier tokens & reserved items — Tank & Healer priority on ties. See the reserved list.

SR link drops in #raid-info every week — check before each raid night.
