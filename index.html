About Us

Who we are, how we operate

Eradication is a community of seasoned players on Nightslayer (US) dedicated to conquering TBC content with precision and intent. We're semi-hardcore — adults with lives, careers, and families — but when raid frames go up, we lock in completely.

"Joke between pulls. Lock in when it counts."

Our focus is efficient progression without the toxic culture that kills guilds. We demand preparation, accountability, and a willingness to improve — and we give the same in return. Guild formed during Classic with veteran leadership and proven TBC credentials.

Loot System

Fair, transparent, progression-minded

We use a Loot Council system tracked through ThatsMyBis. All wishlist data is visible to every raider — no backroom deals. Decisions are made by officers with the guild's long-term progression as the north star.

  • Gear goes where it benefits the raid most — not to the loudest voice
  • Wishlist transparency via ThatsMyBis — priorities are on the record
  • Consistent attendance with high performance builds priority over time
  • New members serve a 2-week trial before full loot eligibility
  • Disputes handled privately with officers — zero drama on raid night
  • Disenchanting for the guild bank is always on the table
